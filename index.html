Father Beaver, tell me a story !

Once upon a time, there was a young boy who lived in a small town with his parents. He loved spending his free time playing games on the computer that his parents had bought for him.

One day, while browsing through the games on his computer, the kid stumbled upon a chess game. He had never played chess before, but he was immediately drawn to the strategic nature of the game.

The kid spent hours each day playing chess on his computer, studying different strategies and practicing against the computer's AI. Over time, he became very good at the game and even started to participate in online chess tournaments.

Read more...

As the kid continued to play and learn more about chess, he also became increasingly interested in the technology behind his computer. He started to explore the different software and programs on his computer, and even taught himself how to code.

Before long, the kid's passion for technology and computer science had grown to rival his love of chess. He spent hours each day tinkering with his computer and learning as much as he could about the field.

The kid's parents were amazed at how quickly he had become so skilled and knowledgeable about computers. They encouraged him to continue pursuing his passion, and even helped him enroll in a program at the local community college to study computer science.

The kid was thrilled to have the opportunity to continue learning and growing in the field that he loved. And even though he still enjoyed playing chess, he knew that his true passion was in the world of computers and technology.

I'm Ilias and this is a story about me.
