Tell me a story !

Once upon a time there was a young boy who was not very good at studies. He was always in the moon, dreaming and lost in thought. His parents and teachers were worried about him because he had a lot of difficulties in class and his academic results were poor.

Read more...

One day, everything changed. The young boy had a click for mathematics. At first, his only goal was to fill his gaps. With time, he discovered some facilities and the material stimulated him.

A spirit of competition was born in him.

Later, he joined an engineering school. Wherever mathematical logic could help him reason, he applied it. Especially in computer science where his curiosity had never finished expanding.

That boy is me. I have learned that, even when we feel at the bottom of the hole and our surroundings never cease to devalue us, work and self-confidence will always allow us to finally achieve ourselves.
